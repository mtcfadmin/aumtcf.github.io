February 2019 Meeting

February 9th 2019 from 6:30 PM onwards

Venue: Hawthorn West Baptist Church, 36 Barton St, Hawthorn VIC 3122

Therefore encourage one another and build one another up, just as you are doing.

1 Thessalonians 5:11

Who we are?

We are inter-denominational christians of Indian background and we meet once a month for worship, bible study, fellowship dinner and other related activities.

What we do?

We strive to support, encourage and grow together towards improving relationship with one another and to serve our Lord & Saviour Jesus Christ.

Where we meet?

We meet in the premises of Hawthorn West Baptist Church, located at 36 Barton Street, Hawthorn, near Hawthorn Station, Melway Ref. 45A9. Please check our events page for more information.
